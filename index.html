ABOUT

I'm a Computer Science Professor (fancy title to say that I teach computer science subjects).
They say if you are passionate about the profession that chose you, everyday at work would seem to be an excursion. I believe that you can prepare employable graduates if only you get hands on with coding, projects and keep evolving with technology changes. You cannot just mentor with ideas, as a faculty you have to be the team lead for your students, working on their project as a senior member, setting the goals, helping them with code, correcting course and be there when they are stuck. Teaching and technology have fascinated me since the graduation years and having spent 19 years in the field of technology education, designing curriculum and managing faculty teams, I feel it's been a fantstic ride.
Here's to the blissful teaching journey!
